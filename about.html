This is all about this page

This is a project which can transform itself according to the human wish as to serve the needs of travellers.Our web application ‘Travel Partner’ aims to improve the usability of bus transportation.A traveller can find the time at which a buses will arrive by providing starting position and destination, sorted on the basis of arrival time.If he needs to enter multiple buses to reach the destination,user will be shown the sequence of buses with the corresponding bus stops at which passenger has to enter and exit from the buses to reach destination.Based on previous user opinions, the chances of high crowd in the bus will be predicted.Users can also use the application to find the route to the most visited places.

  • Scheduling the complete travel till the destination.
  • Review system which could help travellers make right decisions at the right time.
  • Guidance in choosing the best places to visit Restaurants,places with historical importance and all....
